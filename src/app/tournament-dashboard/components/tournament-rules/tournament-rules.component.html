Goalkeeper Rules

Standard (FIFA) — Goalkeeper can handle back-passes from headers and throw-ins.

Strict No Pass Back — Keeper cannot touch the ball with hands from any deliberate kick by a teammate.

Futsal (Hand throw) — Goalkeeper must throw the ball back into play; no kicking from hands.
